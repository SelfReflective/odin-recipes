Panna Cotta von DarkTiger312

Very tasty, is eaten alot by women!!!

Ingredients

  • 500ml Schlagobers
  • 1 Stk Vanilleschote
  • 5 Bl Gelatine
  • 40g Zucker

Steps

  1. Cut the rind from 1/4 lemon into long strips. Place strips in a saucepan with cream and confectioners' sugar. Bring to a simmer over medium heat. Set aside to steep for 10 minutes.
  2. Meanwhile, combine milk and gelatin in a small bowl. Let sit for 5 minutes.
  3. Reheat the cream mixture until hot. Remove from heat. Stir in gelatin mixture and vanilla extract. Remove lemon rind. Pour into 8 ramekins and refrigerate until set, about 1 to 2 hours.
  4. To serve, arrange strawberry slices and raspberries on top of each custard.
